HR Platform

Everything you need to manage people.

Six production-ready modules, working together from day one.

Nigerian Statutory Engine

PAYE across all 6 tax bands, 8% employee pension, 10% employer, NHF, and NSITF — computed to the exact kobo every run.

Bulk Paystack Disbursement

One-click salary transfers via Paystack Transfer API. Pay your entire team in minutes with full audit trail and webhook receipts.

Smart Payslips & Reports

PDF payslips auto-generated post-approval and delivered via email + Termii SMS. Pension schedules and PAYE Form A in Excel.

Leave Management

Annual, sick, and maternity leave with configurable accruals, manager approval flows, and real-time balance tracking per employee.

Employee Records

Full employee profiles with NIN/BVN encryption, department hierarchy, pay grades, and a complete employment lifecycle log.

Multi-Tenant Security

PostgreSQL Row Level Security ensures absolute data isolation between tenants. Every query is scoped by RLS policy — no leakage possible.

Simple process

From onboarding to payday in 3 steps.

01

Register your workspace

Create your tenant workspace. Invite HR admins, configure your pay grades, departments, and statutory settings. Takes under 10 minutes.

02

Add employees

Import or manually add employee records with bank details for Paystack linkage, pay grades, and department assignments.

03

Run payroll & disburse

Initiate a payroll run. The engine computes every statutory deduction. Finance approves. One click sends salary to every bank account.

Payroll engine

Statutory-accurate.
Every single month.

Every PAYE band, every PenCom schedule, every NHF deduction — calculated to the kobo automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual checks.

  • PAYE: 7% – 24% progressive bands per FIRS guidelines
  • Pension: 8% employee + 10% employer (PenCom-compliant)
  • NHF: 2.5% of basic salary — auto-deducted per employee
  • All values stored in kobo — zero floating-point errors
